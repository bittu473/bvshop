Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 11, 2026

Welcome to ShopNest. At ShopNest Pvt Ltd, we are committed to protecting your privacy and securing your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, store, use, and safeguard your details when you use our mobile application and related e-commerce services.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with a customized and luxurious shopping experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • User Profile Details: Your name, email address, phone number, and account avatar.
  • Shipping and Delivery Information: Complete shipping addresses, landmarks, police stations, post offices, districts, postal codes, and digital signatures.
  • Wallet & Transaction History: Records of virtual funds recharge, balance ledger, and purchase transaction histories on our platform.
  • AI Concierge Interactions: Text queries, voice searches, or barcode scans you share with our ShopNest Concierge AI (powered by Google Gemini API).

2. How We Use Your Information

We utilize the collected information to enhance your shopping experience and maintain secure delivery logistics:

  • To process your orders, apply promotional coupon codes, and calculate billing summaries.
  • To manage shipment progression (e.g., handling your "Request Shipment" commands, tracking transit stages).
  • To update your private wallet balance securely upon authorized simulated recharges.
  • To feed relevant contextual info (e.g. search keywords) to the Gemini AI API for intelligent shopping suggestions.
  • To trigger native system notifications keeping you posted on real-time transit milestones.

Note on AI Concierge: Chat content, voice terms, or barcodes scanned during support sessions are securely processed. We do not sell your conversational data to any advertising brokers.

3. Data Protection & Storage

We implement strict security practices to shield your data from unauthorized access:

  • All user profiles, order logs, addresses, and wallet states are persisted locally via an encrypted SQLite database using the high-performance Android Room architecture.
  • Sensitive network components, such as API Keys for Gemini, are strictly managed server-side or compiled securely via BuildConfig variables, avoiding insecure text exposures.
  • No actual credit card numbers or real UPI pins are processed; all checkout recharges are securely handled via safe visual simulations.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We only share your information with third-party service systems in the following circumstances:

  • AI Service Providers: Conversational queries are securely sent to Google Gemini APIs to fetch real-time recommendations.
  • Logistics Partners: Shipment address details are simulated with trusted carriers once you explicitly tap "Request Shipment".
  • Legal Mandates: If required by judicial requests to comply with sovereign Indian commerce regulations.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You maintain ultimate control over your information. At any point inside the ShopNest App, you can:

  • View and edit your personal details in the Profile tab.
  • Manage, update, or completely delete your saved addresses.
  • Clear your Concierge AI chat thread logs at the click of a button.
  • Toggle Dark Mode preferences or switch active application languages instantly.
